Yield
Makes 1 serving

Ingredients

    • 1 spoonful lemon sorbet, softened
    • 1/2 ounce citrus vodka (optional, for a stronger drink)
    • 4 ounces rosé champagne
    • 2 to 3 raspberries, for garnish

Advertisements

Preparation

    1. Combine the sorbet with the vodka in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake vigorously. Add the champagne and stir. Strain into a chilled champagne flute and garnish with floating raspberries.
