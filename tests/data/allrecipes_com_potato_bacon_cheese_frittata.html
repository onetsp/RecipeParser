Potato, Bacon Cheese Frittata

0 made it | 0 reviews | 0 photos
2

Recipe by: kcryss

'I always serve this attractive frittata to overnight guests in our home,' reports Diana Bullock of Hanover, Maryland. 'To cut down on last-minute preparation, I keep cooked bacon in the freezer.'

  1. Boil potatoes in boiling salted water for 5 minutes. Drain and rinse well.
  2. While potatoes are boiling, fry bacon in large frying pan and drain on paper towels.
  3. Remove all but about 2 Tbs bacon grease.
  4. Fry boiled potatoes in remaining bacon grease until brown. Move potatoes aside and add onion. Cook just until clear.
  5. Mix cooked potatoes and onion together and scoop into a greased 2.75 quart baking dish.
  6. In a bowl, beat eggs, milk, butter, 1 cup grated cheese, salt and pepper. Pour over potato/onion mixture.
  7. Sprinkle with crumbled, cooked bacon and 1 cup grated cheese.
  8. Bake, uncovered, at 370 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
